Writing

I see writing as a very important part of my design practice. Usually this is how more complex ideas can be exercised, put together, and recorded. Recently I wrote one of such texts for a publication by Medialab Katowice. In 2015 I also started writing general readership articles about design topics for 2+3d Polish Design Quarterly.

In 2015 I wrote a book chapter titled "Simulation in Creating Cities" in "Data-Driven Methods for City Research and Exploration" edited by Karol Piekarski and published by Medialab Katowice. The text provided theoretical premise for The Reversal of the Roles workshop which I authored and led in Katowice in 2013 together with designer Lina Aue Pogatschnigg. Other authors contributing to the book included Lev Manovich and Dietmar Offenhuber.
