Writing for 2+3d

In 2015 I wrote three articles for 2+3d Polish Design Quarterly. 2+3d is one of the top design magazines in Central Europe. Besides the general topics such as graphic and product design, 2+3d also publishes articles about history of design, design criticism, technology, and creative economies.

In 2016 my book chapter titled "Simulation in Creating Cities" was published in "Data-Driven Methods for City Research and Exploration" by Medialab Katowice. Other authors contributing to the book included Lev Manovich and Dietmar Offenhuber.
