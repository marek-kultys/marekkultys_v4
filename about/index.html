Experience

Science Practice — I have been a Designer at Science Practice since 2012. I led our Sequence Bundles project (for which I wrote a successful Innovate UK funding application), co-authored our research for the Longitude Prize 2014, collaborated with our friends in start-ups in London and Cambridge, and spoke at workshops and meetups.

SP+EE — is a collaboration between Science Practice and Eclipse Experience. We design and research healthcare experiences and interventions. I work at SP+EE on designing user experiences, patient training, and mobile interactions.

bento•bioworks — I joined bento in 2013 when I advised the team at the {% include events/2013-igem.html %}. Since 2014 I have acted as bento's Chief Design Officer developing our lab-in-the-box.

Freelance — I have worked as a freelance designer and design consultant since 2005. I worked for clients including: Immunocore, Applied Wayfinding, Cadbury-Wedel, Polish Ministry of Finance, Warsaw Municipal Office, Millward Brown.

Teaching

I authored and led hands-on visual design workshops for data scientists during IEEEVis workshops in 2012 (Seattle) and 2013 (Atlanta).

I also authored and led a visualisation workshop for young designers at the {% include events/2014-tivc.html %}, and at the MediaLab in Katowice in 2013.

Since 2012 I have guest taught at Chelsea College of Art and Design, City University in London, University of Warsaw, and the Dynamic Media Institute, Massachusetts College of Art and Design in Boston (MassArt).
