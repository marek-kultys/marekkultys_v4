Design-led Research

How do you know if a design solution works for users?

At Science Practice and Ctrl Group, where I work as a designer, we use design-led research methods to answer these questions.

In 2017 I designed and led a two-day hands-on workshop introducing design students to design-led research. During the workshop we scoped design problems, prototyped ideas, and tested solutions with users.

The goal of the workshop was to practice three elements of the design-led method: focus on the right design problem, efficiently build the "most minimal" prototype, and conduct reserch interviews with experts or users.
