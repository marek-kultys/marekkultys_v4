Design Workshops

Since 2012, I've been authoring and facilitating design workshops.

My workshops are all about data and hands-on work. In Paradoxes and Data Explorers I focused on visualisation of data and abstract concepts. More recently, in Design-led Research, I started using the workshop format to introduce participants to design and research methods useful in creating data experiences.
