Paradoxes

A paradox is a seemingly self-contradicing statement, that yet can be true. There are many paradoxes in philosophy and sciences, for example: the Liar Paradox, the Arrow Paradox, the Twin Paradox, or the Fermi Paradox.

"Paradoxes" is a workshop I authored and led during the Thinking in Visual Communication conference in Warsaw in 2014. Workshop task was to use visualisation to explain a selected paradox, and later to propose a speculative visual solution to that paradox. Workshop's focus was on exploring visual strategies for communicating highly complex and abstract concepts.
