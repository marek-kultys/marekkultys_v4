Science Practice and Ctrl Group

Lead Designer May 2013–present

Designer October 2012–May 2013
I currently lead design work on two expert platforms: in genomics and clinical trials. Both projects are at very early stage and we work in an agile way, which means I prototype a lot, and we test frequently. Besides the actual UX and UI design work, I also manage our relationship with clients and partners on several projects. In 2014 I managed to win Innovate UK funding for Science Practice.

bento•bioworks

Product Designer September 2013–November 2014
